Cascade Source: a device to store all your passwords

With Cascade, your passwords are stored in the cloud, your cloud.
Cascade Source is a tiny device connected in your home and used to store your passwords.
Everything on it is encrypted. When getting your passwords, the decryption is only made on your phone, tablet or computer
Using the app Cascade, you will be connected to your Cascade Source and able to read your passwords anywhere in the world.
